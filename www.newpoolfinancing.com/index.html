Financing Your New Swimming Pool is Simple

Paramount Capital is part of the Paramount Pool & Spa Systems family working to help you find a financing program that fits your needs to build your dream pool. If you’re ready to build your your very own backyard resort simply complete the pre-application form. Here is an overview of the services we offer:

We are a FREE Service

Paramount Capital is a FREE service provided to all customers working with an approved authorized Paramount pool builder.

We Don’t Pull Your Credit Report

There’s a simple form to fill out and we DO NOT pull your credit report. We promise.

We Help You Acquire Financing

We are not a lender, but we help you secure loans from $1,000 up to $100,000 to build your backyard resort.

Multiple Lenders

Complete the pre-application and we’ll match you with multiple lenders to help you finance your pool.

24-Hour Notifications

Complete the pre-application and we’ll notify you within 24 hours with multiple lenders to help you finance your pool.

No Equity or Collateral Required

Unsecured financing is available with no equity or collateral required.
